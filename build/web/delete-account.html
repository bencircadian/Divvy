Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your Divvy account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed.

To delete your account:

  1. Send an email to the address below from the email associated with your Divvy account
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete My Account"
  3. Include your account email address in the body of the email
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days

Data that will be deleted:

  • Your account profile and email address
  • All tasks you created
  • Task notes and images you uploaded
  • Your household membership
  • All associated activity history
